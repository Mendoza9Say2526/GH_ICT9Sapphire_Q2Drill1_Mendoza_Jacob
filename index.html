Emergency Go Bag Essentials/Needs

Emergency Water and Food: You need food and water to survive so you do not starve and stay hungry. No matter what, bring this necessary item.
First Aid: You need to heal your wounds after the disaster ends. Make sure you survive with it because that item is another way to sustain yourself.
Radio: Before, during, or after the calamity, listen to the radio for announcements so you can prepare or know what is going to happen next.
Flashlight: You need light in order to see things so you know where you are going all the time and you can escape areas without blindness.
